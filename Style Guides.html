What is living style guide?

A living style guide to better comprehend the quality and mold the look of an application to its designers and developers desires. It contains useful information such as, the color palette theme, logos, layout etc. One of the best things about this is that it is very adaptable and can change as need arises..

Why is a living style guide useful to developers and designers?

The living style guide gives a good vantage point to the designer and developers. One can work independently from the other without having to make up for the slack of the other. It is a very useful tool in terms of project management and definitely increases the overall quality of the work..

My list of living style guide tips

  • Allow yourself wiggle room when determining the time needed for the project.
  • Do not micromanage too much. Try to make a theme and go with the flow.
  • Try to be as clear as you in regards to your design and style choices.
  • Do not se everything in stone. There is always room for improvement.
